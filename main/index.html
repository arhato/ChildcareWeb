About Our Childcare Premise

At our childcare premise, we strive to provide a safe, fun, and nurturing environment for children of all ages. Our experienced staff are dedicated to helping children learn and grow through play, education, and socialization.

Facilities

Our premise includes indoor and outdoor play areas, classrooms, a kitchen for meal preparation, and other amenities to ensure the comfort and safety of children.We accept children from infants to 12 years old.

Curriculum

We offer a range of age-appropriate activities and educational programs to help children develop their physical, cognitive, and social-emotional skills.

Hours of operation

We're open from 7am to 6pm, Monday through Friday.
